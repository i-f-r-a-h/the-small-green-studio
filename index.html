Purpose Led Projects

Pedrick's Zero Waste Shop is local to our studio and we shop there ourselves. We're helping tell the story of how an average family can save tonnes of waste by refilling common containers. We're working on a mini documentary about zero waste shops and their mission, but also sneaking some of this content out onto social media to boost awareness and grow sales to a larger area. We even got to go on a delivery spree in their electric vehicle named "Lavender".
