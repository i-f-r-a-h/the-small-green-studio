Video

Narrative

We're documentary geeks and live to draw out a dynamic narrative from our interviews. And we work carefully to get the best out of our contributors.When we combine all this with cinema and top notch sound design, the results are memorable and magical.

Video

News & Reactive

We have an extended global network of producers and self directing camera operators.This means that when there's little time to plan or a rare opportunity to capture the footage our clients need, we can deploy rapidly and deliver immediately.

photography

Teams Shots.

Show your values and brand personality through your team shots.We're helping lots of brands refresh their photography in light of home and flexible working arrangements. Set your brand free with team shots that reflect this.

photography

Web & Editorial

Make your content pop with an image library of your people and products in their natural habitat.You can spot stock photos a mile off and it takes away from the brand you work hard to maintain.Ask us about imagery for a specific brief, for web or editorial applications.

Event Capture

Conferences, town halls, AGM

Sometimes you want to capture the essence of a conference or event. We can offer a full service of delegate portraits, reportage of speakers and audience participation.We can also gather soundbites and commentary to promote future events or work into a highlights reel.

Event Capture

Outdoor and Sports

We love getting out and about and we're used to capturing the action, even if we have to chase it down.We can help the audience feel like they're part of the action, which usually means getting involved ourselves. Which is no bad thing.

Social Media

Channel audit, management and consultancy

In our experience, we all think we can do social media because we have personal accounts. But as soon as ad budgets, constantly changing algorithms and your own time come in to play, it can be better to outsource it to a specialist.

Social Media

Content creation

It can be tempting to use templated creation tools too, but how do you stand out with so many also doing this? With any brand, engagement and interaction are key and we know lots of ways to help.
